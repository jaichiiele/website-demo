The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand is a Senior High School academic track in the Philippines designed for students pursuing careers in business, finance, accounting, and entrepreneurship. It focuses on building foundational knowledge in management, marketing, financial literacy, and operations through specialized subjects and practical, real-world simulations.
